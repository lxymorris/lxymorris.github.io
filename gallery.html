Sixth Period

Sixth period is an explanation of what coming to age in the current social and political climate is like and how that has impacted our lives. A voicing of frustrations that have been building. Frustrations from being told we're "too young" to understand the politics around us while still being expected to deal with their consequences. Of feeling uncared for after being shown time and time again that money and political favor are worth more than our health and safety. Of voicing opinions only to be talked over and dismissed.

To my classmates, colleagues, and younger siblings...you're not alone, your anger is justified, and one way or another we will change things

To our parents, teachers, and role models...we thank you for giving us room to grow, amplifying our voices, and supporting us in our fight for improvement.

And to those of you who may feel like this message is directed at you...ask yourselves if you're really listening to the voices of the young people around you. We may just be kids, students, and young adults now, but just you wait. We're coming for you.

We've reached the final class period of the day. It's been a long one. But we've learned a lot. Not only from our textbooks but from being confronted by the real world as well. We intend to put that knowledge to good use.
